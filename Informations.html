Informations About Yamii

What is Yamii?

Yamii is an AI-powered assistant developed for educational purposes manage interactions across popular platforms such as WhatsApp, Instagram, and Discord.

Key Features

  • Social Media Integration: Automates messaging and notifications on various platforms.
  • Personalized Responses: Learns user preferences for tailored interactions.
  • Multilingual Support: Communicates in English and Italian.
  • Entertainment: Includes fun games like Rock Paper Scissors with more to come!

Future Updates

We are constantly improving Yamii. Planned features include:

  • New games and interactive tools.
  • Support for additional platforms and languages.
  • Advanced AI features for better personalization and automation.
